About Me

Hi there, my name is Connor and I am a web development student at Houston Coding Bootcamp. I was born and raised in Houston TX and somehow never got tried of the year around one hundred percent humidity. I first became interested in web development after my older brother showed me the dev tools in Google Chrome when I was younger. I realized that with only a few lines of code and an understanding of what was possible, I could make anything I wanted.

When I am is not writing code, I am often prepping to DM a weekly game of Dungeons & Dragons for my friends. Some of my other hobbies include: graphic design, cycling, reading, and drinking good coffee.
